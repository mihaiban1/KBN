Who We Are

Building Kingdom businesses that honor God and serve communities across Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

To be the leading Kingdom Capital Fund in Africa, empowering Christian entrepreneurs and organizations to build sustainable businesses that reflect biblical values and create lasting impact in their communities.

Our Mission

To equip and partner with churches, Christian non-profit organizations, and marketplace leaders to develop ethical, Spirit-led business leaders who operate with Kingdom principles and create generational wealth for God's glory.

Our Core Values

Intentional

We operate with strategic focus, biblical values, measurable goals, and active discipleship. Every decision we make is purposeful and aligned with God's kingdom principles.

International

We build cross-border partnerships, foster resource-sharing, and extend our influence across nations to create a global network of Kingdom-minded business leaders.

Generational

We empower all ages to build legacy and sustainability. Our approach creates lasting impact that benefits current and future generations through mentorship and wealth building.

Our Structure & Programs

Local Churches

Equip and partner with churches to develop Marketplace entrepreneurs who can be the church in the marketplace rather than just within the four walls.

KBN Church Business Small Groups

Small groups within churches that develop Christian entrepreneurs through fellowship, training, and mutual accountability.

Kingdom Stewardship Training

Comprehensive training on biblical principles of money, business, and leadership for church members and leaders.

Church Impact Fund

Micro-loans specifically designed for church members with income-generating ideas that align with their church's mission.

Christian Non-Profit Organizations

Strengthen operational capacity and create sustainable funding streams to give back to their communities more effectively.

Capacity Building Hub

Workshops and training sessions on stewardship, marketplace leadership, and organizational development.

Social Enterprise Incubator

Support non-profits in building sustainable business arms that generate revenue while fulfilling their mission.

Grant Matching Program

Connect nonprofits with faith-based donors and partners to maximize funding opportunities and impact.

Christian Entrepreneurs / Marketplace Leaders

Cultivate a network of ethical, Spirit-led business leaders who operate with Kingdom principles in the marketplace.

KBN Marketplace Chapters

Local and international groups for training, networking, and accountability among Christian business leaders.

Kingdom Capital Fund

Seed capital and comprehensive investment coaching for established Christian entrepreneurs ready to scale.

Mentorship Tracks

Generational mentorship connecting seasoned business leaders with new entrepreneurs for wisdom transfer.
